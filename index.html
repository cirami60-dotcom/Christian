Mental Health and Addiction in Harrisonburg

This can happen to anyone. We need to talk about it.

This Is Real

Eddie, a loyal friend and co-worker, is struggling with untreated anxiety and depression. Amber, our former manager, always smiling, lost her job after testing positive for meth. These aren’t isolated stories—they show a bigger issue in Harrisonburg.

The Connection

"Many individuals who abuse drugs are also diagnosed with mental disorders and vice versa." – DBHDS

Untreated mental health often leads to substance use as a form of coping. Without integrated care, both issues spiral.

The Local Gap

Organizations like HRCSB and Blue Ridge Free Clinic are doing important work, but services remain separate and underfunded.
